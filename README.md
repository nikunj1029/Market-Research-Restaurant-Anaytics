# Market-Research-Restaurant-Anaytics

This research aims to analyze the restaurant market in Pune to identify the best strategy for launching a food delivery startup. The objective is to understand customer demand, competition, pricing trends, and market gaps to make data-driven business decisions.

 Problem Statement
  A food delivery startup wants to enter the Pune market but faces key questions:
	•	Where should the business be launched?
	•	What type of food should be offered?
	•	What pricing strategy should be followed?
	•	What is the level of competition in different areas?
	•	How can the startup differentiate itself?

 Data Collection
The dataset used includes:
	•	Restaurant details (name, locality, cuisines)
	•	Ratings and votes (customer engagement)
	•	Pricing (charges for two)
	•	Food type (Veg / Non-Veg / Jain / Halal)
	•	Alcohol availability
	•	Operational timings

 Data Processing (ELT Approach)
The project followed an ELT (Extract, Load, Transform) approach:
 Extract
	•	Data collected from public datasets
 Load
	•	Loaded into SQL database
 Transform
	•	Data cleaning in SQL & Power BI:
	•	Removed nulls and duplicates
	•	Standardized price column
	•	Cleaned timing data
	•	Created calculated columns (Price bucket, Time slot, Alcohol label

 Page 2: Location & Demand Opportunity
Analysis included:
	•	Demand by locality
	•	Revenue potential
	•	Supply vs demand
 Key Insight:
	•	Areas like Baner, Hinjewadi, Viman Nagar show high demand
	•	Some areas show high demand but controlled competition
⸻
 Page 3: Customer Preference & Product Strategy
Analysis included:
	•	Food type preference
	•	Price sensitivity
	•	Time-based demand
	•	Alcohol availability
 Key Insights:
	•	Non-Veg dominates demand
	•	Medium pricing attracts most customers
	•	Peak demand during lunch & evening
	•	Alcohol availability is a potential differentiator
  
Page 4: Market Entry Strategy
Advanced strategic analysis:
	•	High revenue localities
	•	Market maturity (new vs established)
	•	Budget vs premium distribution
	•	High-rated vs low-rated competition
 Key Insights:
	•	Market has a mix of established and low-visibility restaurants
	•	Several areas have low-rated restaurants → opportunity zones
	•	Medium price segment dominates
  
 Key Findings
 Location Strategy
	•	Best areas to start:
	•	Baner
	•	Hinjewadi
	•	Viman Nagar
 Reason:
	•	High demand
	•	Strong revenue potential
	•	Balanced competition

Product Strategy
	•	Focus on:
	•	Non-Veg food
	•	Popular cuisines (North Indian, Fast Food)

 Pricing Strategy
	•	Target:
	•	Medium price segment
 Reason:
	•	Highest customer demand
	•	Balance between affordability & profit
  
   Time Strategy
	•	Focus operations on:
	•	Lunch (12–3 PM)
	•	Evening (7–10 PM)

 Differentiation Strategy
	•	Alcohol availability (selectively)
	•	Better quality than low-rated competitors
	•	Faster delivery

 Business Recommendation
 The startup should launch in Baner or Hinjewadi because:
 Why these locations?
	•	High customer demand (votes)
	•	Strong revenue potential
	•	Not fully saturated
 Benefits of launching here:
	1.	Faster customer acquisition
	2.	Higher order volume
	3.	Better revenue generation
	4.	Competitive advantage over low-rated restaurants
